What this tool is (and isn’t)

This tool provides

  1. An approximate UK→US GPA estimate

The score is not an official “acceptance probability”. It is a practical indicator based on typical expectations for MS CS tiers plus your selected profile strengths.

FAQ

Is a UK 2:1 good enough for MS CS in the USA?

Often yes. For top tiers, strong research/projects and strong letters can matter a lot.

Is 68% a good grade for MS CS in the USA?

68% is usually a high 2:1 and is viewed positively by many programs.

Do US universities convert UK grades to a GPA?

Some convert internally, some use credential evaluators, and some assess holistically.

Do MS CS programs care more about CS module grades or overall grades?

CS + maths module performance often matters more, but overall performance still matters.

Can projects and internships offset a slightly lower GPA?

Sometimes yes. Strong proof of ability can improve competitiveness.

Is this an official conversion?

No, it’s a guide. Always check each university’s evaluation approach.
